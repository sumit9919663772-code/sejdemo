Leucoderma (Vitiligo)

Ayurvedic understanding of leucoderma — causes, progression, lifestyle care and long-term balance.

What is Leucoderma?

Leucoderma, also known as vitiligo, is a condition where the skin loses its natural pigmentation and white patches appear.

It is non-contagious and does not spread through touch.

How Does Leucoderma Start?

It often begins with small pale patches that slowly expand. Ayurveda links it to digestive and immune imbalance.

What To Do

  • Maintain strong digestion and routine
  • Follow a compatible, balanced diet
  • Manage stress and mental health
  • Stay consistent with long-term care

What To Avoid

  • Incompatible food combinations
  • Irregular eating and sleeping habits
  • Frequent treatment changes
  • Fear, panic and stress

How Ayurveda Helps

Ayurveda focuses on correcting digestion, improving immunity and restoring internal balance, which supports natural pigmentation over time.
