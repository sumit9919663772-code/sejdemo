Privacy Policy

At SEJ Ayurveda, your personal and health information is treated with the highest level of confidentiality, ethics and care, in alignment with Ayurvedic principles.

At SEJ Ayurveda, we are committed to protecting the privacy, confidentiality and integrity of personal and health-related information shared with us through our website, consultations and treatment services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, age, gender and contact details
  • Mobile number, email address and communication preferences
  • Health history and symptoms shared during consultation
  • Order, payment and delivery-related information
  • Website usage data for performance and improvement

2. Ayurvedic & Medical Information

Health information shared during consultation is collected solely for Ayurvedic assessment and guidance.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide Ayurvedic consultation and guidance
  • To process and deliver treatment kits
  • For follow-ups and care support
  • To improve our services

4. Payment & Financial Data

All payments are processed through secure third-party gateways. We do not store card or banking details.

5. Data Protection

We apply reasonable technical and administrative safeguards to protect your information.

This Privacy Policy reflects SEJ Ayurveda’s commitment to trust, ethics and responsible data handling.
