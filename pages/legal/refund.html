Refund & Cancellation Policy

Transparent policies for consultations and Ayurvedic products

At SEJ Ayurveda, we follow clear and transparent policies regarding consultations, treatment kits, cancellations and refunds. Please read the following carefully before placing an order.

Consultation Fees

  • Audio consultation fee (₹300) is fully prepaid.
  • Video consultation fee (₹500) is fully prepaid.
  • Once booked, consultation fees are non-refundable.

Product Orders – Full Payment

Orders placed with full payment are processed after confirmation. Refunds may be considered only if the order has not been dispatched.

Product Orders – Partial Payment

  • An advance payment of ₹150 is required.
  • The advance amount is non-refundable.
  • Remaining amount is payable at delivery.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellation requests must be raised at the earliest. Approval depends on the current order status.

Damaged or Incorrect Products

Please inform our support team promptly. Each case is reviewed individually.

No Guarantee Disclaimer

Ayurvedic products support natural balance. Refunds are not provided based on personal expectations or outcomes.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed through the original payment method within a reasonable timeframe.

This policy is shared for customer transparency and awareness.
