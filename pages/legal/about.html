Understanding Skin Disorders Deeply

Conditions like psoriasis, vitiligo, eczema, acne, and chronic fungal infections are not just skin-deep. They are deeply connected to liver function, digestion, blood purity, and mental stress.

Ayurveda looks at these conditions through dosha imbalance. Pitta, Kapha, and Vata play a major role. Without balancing these, no medicine can give long-term relief.
